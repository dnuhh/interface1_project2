River Safety

Your safety and comfort is absolutely our first priority. The following is information is not an indication of absolute safety in any conditions. Use these guidelines before you enter any boat.
Canoeing the Grand provides the following services at no extra charge, upon request:
  • dry land basic canoe enter/exit
  • paddling and safety communication instruction
  • river hazards awareness literature
  • personal flotation device (life jacket) and safety kit (includes bailer, rope and whistle)
