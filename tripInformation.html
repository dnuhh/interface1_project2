Trip Information

Canoeing The Grand is ideally situated on the banks of the Grand River. Our trips offer a range of opportunities for both the novice paddler and to those wanting a more vigorous outing. We provide worry free direct access to the river and proximity to many more points along nearly 100 km of river trails. Although we specialize in shuttled trips, canoes are also available for hourly or daily rental.
Every canoe trip price includes two personal floatation devices (PFDs), canoe, paddles, parking and shuttle service. We provide service by reservation (strongly recommended), and drop in.

OUR BOATS

Get ready to be moved whether by canoe, kayak or tube. We are proud to share our heritage river and its pastoral scenery at every turn with our guests. the gentle moving waters make for a safe and relaxing adventure. Our longest trips are suitable for those wanting to test their grit or combine their paddle time with a camping trip. Our shorter journeys can be extended with some fishing swimming or a picnic. It is your day, your way! Booking is simple, you arrive 20 minutes prior to departure time, we shuttle you to your start and you paddle back to our location. Nothing could be more simple or relaxing!

Tubing

Canoeing the Grand is an experience that is both iconic and peaceful. As you quietly paddle our beautiful heritage river you will discover our region from a whole new vantage point.

Canoeing the Grand uses Old Town 15'8" canoes.
Specs:
Weight: 80 lbs. Capacity: 800 lbs, 2 adults and 2 children age 3-12, or 3 adults.

Kayaking is an easy, safe way to navigate our river. Our ocean kayaks are roomy, stable, have adjustable foot braces and are easy to maneuver. Kayaks allow you to discover the river as a group or give you the freedom to adventure solo.

Canoeing the Grand uses Old Town Otter XT 9'6" kayaks.
Specs:
Adult Single Weight: 39 lbs. 9' Capacity: 275 lbs, 1 person.
Adult Tandem Weight: 61 lbs. 14' Capacity: 500 lbs, 2 persons.
Kids Single Weight: 29 lbs. 7'5" Capacity: 115 lbs, 1 person.

Tubing is for those that like to take it slow! It is a perfect way to enjoy the river with friends and family when time is on your side. The tubes are sturdy, comfortable, and hook easily together for that “group experience”.

Add a cooler tube for hauling your picnic cooler and you have a floating family picnic, perfect for a hot summer cool down!
