Our Boats

Find Out More About Our Boats

Canoe

Canoeing the grand is an experience that is both iconic and peaceful. As you quietly paddle our beautiful heritage river you will discover our region from a whole new vantage point.
Canoeing the Grand uses Old Town 15'8" canoes. That are flat-bottom, stable crafts, ideal for novice or recreational use. Canoe Specifications Weight: 80 lbs. Capacity: 800 lbs, 2 adults and 2 children age 3-12, or 3 adults.

Kayaks

Kayaking is an easy, safe way to navigate our river. Our ocean kayaks are roomy, stable, have adjustable foot braces and are easy to maneuver. Kayaks allow you to discover the river as a group or give you the freedom to adventure solo.

Canoeing the Grand uses Old Town Otter XT 9'6" kayaks. That are flat-bottom, stable crafts, ideal for novice or recreational use. Adult Single Weight: 39 lbs. 9' Capacity: 275 lbs, 1 person. Adult Tandem Weight: 61 lbs. 14' Capacity: 500 lbs, 2 persons. Kids Single Weight: 29 lbs. 7'5" Capacity: 115 lbs, 1 person.

tubes

Tubing is for those that like to take it slow! It is a perfect way to enjoy the river with friends and family when time is on your side. The tubes are sturdy, comfortable, and hook easily together for that “group experience”.
